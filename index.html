🎯 What this is

A private Slack community for engineers, product folks, founders and operators mostly in Berlin – with channels for Munich and Hamburg too. It’s a safe, low-noise space to stay plugged into what’s actually worth your time.

Why people join

  • Honest, backchannel chat about jobs, hiring and navigating the tech scene
  • A cosy place to share your launches, prototypes and side projects
  • Deep-dive conversations about AI, product, engineering and building in public
  • Small, thoughtful community instead of yet another massive Discord

💬 What we talk about

Most days feel like a good hallway track: people share what they’re building, ask for feedback, and swap practical notes on working in tech in Berlin, Munich and Hamburg.

Berlin AI, deep-tech, product, founders
Munich B2B, enterprise, hardware, mobility
Hamburg startups, community, creative tech

There are channels for launches, jobs, “building in public” logs, and nerdy deep dives – plus the occasional thread about what’s happening offline in each city.

👥 Who it’s for

  • Software engineers, data/ML folks, designers and product managers
  • Founders and operators building in or around tech
  • People moving to Berlin / Munich / Hamburg who want to plug into the scene

The vibe is: warm, curious, a bit nerdy, no hustle porn. Think “post-conference hallway track”, every day.
