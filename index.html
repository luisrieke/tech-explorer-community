Berlin Tech Explorer Community

A cosy, curated space for people who love building and exploring tech in Germany’s most vibrant cities.

🎯 What this is

A private Slack community for engineers, product folks, founders and operators mostly in Berlin – with channels for Munich and Hamburg too. It’s a safe, low-noise space to stay plugged into what’s actually worth your time.

Why people join

  • Weekly curated list of the best tech events in Berlin, Munich and Hamburg
  • Honest, backchannel chat about jobs and hiring (without recruiter spam)
  • A cosy place to share your launches and side projects
  • Small, thoughtful community instead of yet another massive Discord

📡 Event radar

Every week we ship a hand-picked selection of meetups, talks, hack nights and niche gatherings for each city. Less FOMO, more signal.

Berlin deep-tech, AI, product, founders
Munich B2B, enterprise, hardware, mobility
Hamburg startups, media, community meetups

We also hang out in event threads to see who’s going, share notes afterwards, and spin up spontaneous coffee chats.

👥 Who it’s for

  • Software engineers, data/ML folks, designers and product managers
  • Founders and operators building in or around tech
  • People moving to Berlin / Munich / Hamburg who want to plug into the scene

The vibe is: warm, curious, a bit nerdy, no hustle porn. Think “post-conference hallway track”, every day.
